Welcome to the Waste Management Dashboard

An interactive platform to explore waste production trends across various sectors and regions.

Background and Introduction

The Waste Management Dashboard is an innovative platform dedicated to visualizing and analyzing waste generation trends across various sectors and regions. Built on meticulously prepared OECD waste management data, it aims to shed light on the growing global waste crisis, foster awareness, and promote sustainable waste reduction practices.

Aim and Objectives

Aim: To create an accessible, visually appealing dashboard that provides actionable insights into waste generation and its environmental impacts.

Visualize Trends: Highlight waste production trends across sectors like manufacturing, agriculture, and energy generation.

Promote Awareness: Educate users about the scale and impact of waste generation.

Support Sustainability: Encourage sustainable practices by showcasing recovery and recycling rates.

Interactivity: Provide an engaging user experience with interactive visualizations.

Waste Memorial

This website visualizes the amount of waste created over time, providing an interactive platform to explore trends in waste production across different regions and sectors.

It highlights the growth of waste generation, offering insights into its environmental impact and promoting awareness around sustainability and waste reduction efforts.
